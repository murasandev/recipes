Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice (2-3 lemons)
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup of parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • Ground pepper

Salad

  • 1 cup of quinoa
  • 1 cup of feta
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 - 3 avocados
  • Cherry tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Bring uncooked quinoa and water to a boil.
  2. Lower heat to a simmer until water is gone.
  3. Let quinoa cool.
  1. Dice veggies and mix in a bowl.
  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, salt, garlic, and honey in a bowl.
  2. Mix quinoa and veggies with dressing.
  3. Mix in the feta and avocado.
  4. Add pepper to taste.
  5. Let salad chill.
  6. Serve.
