Quinoa Salad

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1/4 cup of lemon juice (2-3 lemons)
  • 1 tablespoon of red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup of parsley
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • Ground pepper

Salad

  • 1 cup of quinoa
  • 1 cup of feta
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 - 3 avocados
  • Cherry tomatoes
