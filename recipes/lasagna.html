Lamb Bechamel Lasagna

Ingredients:

Lasagna Noodles

  • 21/2 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Large Egg Yolks
  • Pinch of Salt

Bolognese Sauce

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Grated Onion
  • 2 Grated Carrot
  • 2 Lb of Meat (Lamb or Beef or Half and Half)
  • 4-6 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine (Merlot Pref)
  • 2-3 Cans of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Worcestershire
  • Heavy Cream

Bechamel Sauce

  • 5 Tablespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 Cups of Cream
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 2 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg

Toppings

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley

Instructions:

Lasagna Noodles

  1. Mound flour on a large pastry board.
  2. Break eggs and yolks into the well and add the salt.
  3. Whisk egg with a fork inside flour mound.
  4. Incorporate the eggs & flour by slowly bringing more flour in from the inside edges of the well.
  5. Continue mixing the flour and the eggs until no longer runny.
  1. Using hands, bring in the outside edges.
  2. Knead the ball of dough as you would bread, pushing down with the heel of your hand.
  3. Knead until smooth and satiny. (About 5 - 7 min)
  4. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let sit for 30 min.
  1. Roll pasta out into 1/8 inch thick.
  2. Cut into 12 inch long strips.
  3. Cook in boiling water for 30 secs.
  4. Place cooked pieces into ice water.
  5. Dry and set aside on kitchen towels.

Bolognese Sauce

  1. Mince garlic and saute in oil.
  2. Grate onions and carrots
  3. Cook onion and carrots for a few min in garlic and oil.
  4. Make well with carrots and onions in pot.
  5. Cook down wine in well (Optional).
  6. Add meat to the middle of the well and cook.
  7. While meat is cooking, add tomato puree to meat and cook.
  8. Add chopped tomatoes.
  9. Add Worcestershire sauce.
  10. Cook for a few minutes.
  11. Add heavy cream (Optional).
  12. Let sauce simmer for 15+ minutes.

Bechamel Sauce

  1. On medium heat, warm butter until melted.
  2. Gradually add flour and stir with a wooden spoon until smooth.
  3. Cook for 2-3 min, continue stirring to avoid burning.
  4. In seperate pot, heat cream until almost boiling.
  5. Combine cream to butter mix 1/2 cup at a time while constantly whisking.
  6. Cook 12 min stirring constantly.
  7. Take off heat.
  8. Stir in 1 egg yolk at a time.
  9. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  10. Mix with ricotta, mozarella, and parmesian cheese (Optional).

Lasagna

  1. Spread thin layer of sauce on bottom of baking dish to prevent sticking.
  2. Lay down layer of lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread layer of bechamel sauce.
  4. Spread layer of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat steps 1 - 4 til done.
  1. Top final layer with noodles, sauce and mozarella/parmesian cheese.
  2. Cover with foil.
  3. Bake for approximately one hour at 350-475 degrees.
  4. Bake for 15-30 minutes with foil.
  5. Remove foil for remainder of cook time.
  6. Top with parsley and basil.
  7. Let lasagna sit for 10-20 minutes, to settle.
