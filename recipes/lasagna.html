Lamb Bechamel Lasagna

Ingredients:

Bolognese Sauce

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Grated Onion
  • 2 Grated Carrot
  • 2 Lb of Meat (Lamb or Beef or Half and Half)
  • 4-6 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine (Merlot Pref)
  • 2-3 Cans of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Worcestershire
  • Heavy Cream

Bechamel Sauce

  • 5 Tablespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 Cups of Cream
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 2 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg
