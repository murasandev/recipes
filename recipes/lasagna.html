Lamb Bechamel Lasagna

Ingredients:

Lasagna Noodles

  • 21/2 Cups of All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Large Egg Yolks
  • Pinch of Salt

Bolognese Sauce

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Grated Onion
  • 2 Grated Carrot
  • 2 Lb of Meat (Lamb or Beef or Half and Half)
  • 4-6 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • Tomato Paste
  • Red Wine (Merlot Pref)
  • 2-3 Cans of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Worcestershire
  • Heavy Cream

Bechamel Sauce

  • 5 Tablespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 4 Cups of Cream
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 2 Teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Nutmeg

Toppings

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Parsley

Instructions:

Lasagna Noodles

  1. Mound flour on a large pastry board.
  2. Break eggs and yolks into the well and add the salt.
  3. Whisk egg with a fork inside flour mound.
  4. Incorporate the eggs & flour by slowly bringing more flour in from the inside edges of the well.
  5. Continue mixing the flour and the eggs until no longer runny.
  1. Using hands, bring in the outside edges.
  2. Knead the ball of dough as you would bread, pushing down with the heel of your hand.
  3. Knead until smooth and satiny. (About 5 - 7 min)
  4. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let sit for 30 min.
  1. Roll pasta out into 1/8 inch thick.
  2. Cut into 12 inch long strips.
  3. Cook in boiling water for 30 secs.
  4. Place cooked pieces into ice water.
  5. Dry and set aside on kitchen towels.
