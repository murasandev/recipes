Smoked Ribs

Ingredients

  • Yellow Mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cinnamon
  • Smoke Paprika
  • Brown Sugar
  • 1 Stick of Butter per rack

Preparing Rack

  1. Remove membrane to allow rub to penetrate all sides of the meat
  2. Trim any excess fat / hard fat that will take a long time to render
  3. Pat rack dry
  4. Cover rack with yellow mustard
  5. Apply rub to rack

Cooking

  1. Preheat smoker to 250o
  2. Put ribs on smoker for 3 hours
  3. Sprits to maintain moisture about every hour
  4. After 3 hours wrap ribs in tin foil
  5. Set back on smoker for 1 more hour

Tin Foil Wrap

  1. Lay tin foil down
  2. Lay a layer of brown sugar on foil, about size of 1 rack
  3. Add slices of butter on top of brown sugar
  4. Meat side down, put 1 rack on top of the brown sugar and butter
  5. Add brown sugar and butter to the bone side of the rack
  6. Sprits rack before wrapping ribs
  7. Wrap and close tin foil so rack will steam
  8. Continue with the remainder of the racks
