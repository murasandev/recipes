Smoked Prime Rib

Ingredients

Cooking

  1. Preheat smoker to 500o
  2. Put prime rib in smoker for 15 minutes
  3. After 15 mins, open smoker to let grill cool down
  4. Set temp to 325o and continue to smoke meat
    *Based on 3-5 lb roast, expect 1-2 hours to cook
  5. Smoke prime rib until internal temperature reaches 130o
  6. Set on cutting board and drape tin foil over prime rib
  7. Let sit for 20 min
  8. Cut and serve
    9. *spritz prime rib about every 30-45 min with water
