Caesar Salad Dressing Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 small garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon anchovy paste
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire
  • 1 cup of mayo
  • 1/2 cup of parmigiano-reggiano
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
10 servings

1 teaspoon anchovy paste = 2 filets

1 tablespoon = 3 teaspoons

Instructions

  1. Whisk together the garlic, anchovy paste, lemon juice, dijon mustard, and Worcestershire.
  2. Add the mayo, parmigiano-reggiano, salt and pepper and whisk until well combined.
  3. Adjust to your liking, and enjoy!
