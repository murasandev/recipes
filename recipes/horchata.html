Horchata Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of long grain rice
  • 3 cups of hot water
  • 1 3 inch long Ceylon soft cinnamon stick broken into pieces
  • 1/2 cup of sugar or 1 can of condensed milk
  1. Combine all Ingredients in a container or bowl.
  2. Stir to dissolve sugar, cover, and let rest overnight or for up to 24 hours.
  3. Pour entire mixture into a blender and blend on high speed until rice is pulverized, about a minute.
  4. Strain through nut milk bag into a bowl and refrigerate.
  5. Stir before serving.
